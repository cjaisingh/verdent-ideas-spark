## Overall read

The project is a well-structured operator platform with genuine architectural discipline — four load-bearing invariants intact, comprehensive RLS coverage, and a thorough CI suite. The dominant risk is significant scope accumulation: the `[Unreleased]` CHANGELOG section contains Gemini TTS, the Rork iPhone companion, overnight phase queues, capability promotion workflows, and Phase 5/6/6b task expansion — all well beyond Phase 2's stated remit of "operator control surface." A secondary but concrete risk is that all GitHub Actions workflows use semver tags (`@v4`, `@v3`, `@v2`) rather than SHA digests, leaving the supply chain open to tag mutation across 15 workflow files.
